Welcome New Members!

Join us as we enable people to make things using technology. This is a hands-on club. You'll be designing and building your own tech projects with a bunch of great folks who love to share insights and get their hands dirty making things.

Remember, we build things! So over time, you will have your own collection of useful & fun things that you have learned to build and in doing so, you'll know how to invent things of your own.

JUMPING IN

Being a technology club has its challenges. One of them is
ensuring that everyone can get the most out of meetings even
as newcomers. So, we've come up with a process for you to
join the conversation, learn at your own pace, and to
get help when needed.


01 JOIN THE CONVERSATION

Since our primary communications to members is via email and forums, participating electronically is crucial to membership. You will be lost if you ignore club emails and only attend meetings.

Please join us by filling out our MEMBERSHIP FORM.

02 LEARN AT YOUR OWN PACE

Using On-Ramps is how we bring new members up to speed. An On-Ramp is a collection of presentations or exercises that will help integrate terms, tools, and common practices used by the club into your skill set.

There are separate On-Ramps for Small Computers and 3D Printing. Members are strongly urged to familiarize themselves with the ON-RAMP MATERIAL for a richer club experience.

03 GET HELP WHEN NEEDED

Learning at your own pace shouldn't be frustrating. Whether you are working on an On-Ramp exercise or your own project, we offer help. Attend a Catch-Up Session (CUS) or ask for help on our forums. There are separate forums for Small Computers and 3D Printing.

See the CLUB CALENDAR for regularly scheduled CUS sessions.
