New Member On-Ramp.

On-Ramps are a collection of presentations selected to help new members come up to speed quickly.

The Small Computer On-Ramp is the more involved of the two, but if any of the terms ESP8266,
Arduino, MQTT, & Home Assistant are unfamiliar, you will benefit from the series.

Small Computers On-Ramp

The Small Computer On-Ramp series is a sampler of small computer technologies. It won't make you an expert in anything, but should whet your appetite for what is possible.

The series starts with simple programming and then expands to building sensors and automation decisions based on sensor data.
